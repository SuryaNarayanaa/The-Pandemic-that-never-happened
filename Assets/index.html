Deadly Thanatos Virus Spreads Worldwide

March 5, 2025 – Global Health Alert

A new, highly lethal virus known as Thanatos has emerged, sending shockwaves across the globe. Originating from an undisclosed region, the virus has begun its rapid spread, leaving scientists scrambling for answers.

Governments worldwide have declared states of emergency, enforcing strict lockdowns and deploying the military to maintain order. Reports of mass evacuations, riots, and food shortages are emerging.

Some speculate that Thanatos may have been engineered as a bioweapon, while others fear an alien origin. Online sources claim evidence of government cover-ups.
